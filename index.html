Our Ongoing Projects

Discover the initiatives we are actively running to support our communities.

Community Feeding

Preparing and distributing nutritious meals and essential rations to underprivileged communities, ensuring no one goes to sleep hungry.

Relief Distribution

Our dedicated volunteers working on the ground to pack and distribute essential supplies, providing immediate relief during critical times.

Night Outreach

Conducting night drives to provide food, blankets, and support to the homeless population living on the streets.

Animal Welfare

Caring for voiceless street animals by regularly conducting feeding drives and ensuring stray dogs receive proper nutrition and care.
